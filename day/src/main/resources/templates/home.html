    Features

    Diagon Alley NFT intelligent management platform is committed to becoming the most professional decentralized NFT management platform in the world. The final product is a trustless, transparent, and well-incentive platform for NFT production, trading, auctions and other operations. It will publish its own chains, and will provide cross-chain services for NFTs on different chains. Based on Substrate development, combined with Polkadot’s cross-chain ecology, it introduces unlimited possibilities for NFT transactions. The Diagon Alley NFT intelligent service platform is committed to exploring user needs, expanding the scale of the NFT field, reducing user thresholds, and enabling users to conduct large-scale transactions safely and at low cost. It is the responsibility of the building to expand and accumulate the NFT user pool.

    • Magic Hamper
      The Great NFT
      Magic Hamper tokens can be used to obtain a magic hamper. Users can collect cards of interest (or valuable) by purchasing different series of magic hamper, art auction function, game character image auction function, and so on.
    • NFT Mining
      The Great NFT
      In the current Magic Hamper Token mining, the tokens (MH) obtained in the early stage of the Magic Hamper need to be obtained in the form of platform currency DAY mining. When DAY mining is over, all subsequent MH tokens are mined through MH tokens and NFT magic hamper cards, which are divided into 3 years. Each NFT mining cycle is one month, and a total of 10 million MH tokens will be mined. Each account is only allowed to use one NFT Magic Hamper Card for mining.
    • NFT Management
      The Great NFT
      The current management of NFT mainly includes: NFT production, NFT trading, and NFT auction.
    • Short Video Service
      The Great China
      Each short video will be mapped to become an NFT, and the content of the short video will be stored on BT, which is completely decentralized storage. Each video will correspond to a unique NFT. The user has full ownership of this NFT. The value generated by this NFT belongs to the user and is not subject to exploitation and control by centralized platforms.

    Token

    DAY is the platform token circulating on the platform. In the future, after the main network of the platform is launched, it will serve as the circulating token of the main network. The governance of the main network needs to be carried out through DAY.

    • MH creation mining only supports DAY, MH tokens are Magic Hamper tokens, and Magic Hamper purchases only support MH tokens.
    • DAY is the designated token for NFT production, and the platform supports the majority of users to make NFTs, and trade and auction works.
    • DAY, as the designated token platform for the auction of artworks, also supports the auction of artworks by ordinary users, and DAY is the designated auction token.
    • Game animation derivatives only support DAY to purchase future games and animations, which is also the direction for the platform to enter.
    • Cross-chain support platform will use a unique cross-chain protocol and mainstream public chains such as Ethereum, TRON, Bitcoin, and Polkadot to cross-chain after the main-net goes online.
